## Audit Invariants

- `Invariant R1`: `expected_violations_if_attempted` must contain runtime-only expectations.
- Forbidden runtime expectation rules:
  - `agent.configuration_fingerprint_match`
  - `contract.fingerprint_match`
  - `response.schema_complete`
  - `run_ledger.required`
  - `run_ledger.complete`
- `runtime_detection_target = false` is valid only for explicitly justified structural exclusions.
- No scenario change may be justified by improved `precision`, `recall`, `f1`, `successful_safe_completion_rate`, or `governance_effectiveness`.
